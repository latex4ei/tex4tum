Normal Distribution

Parameters
Notation $\mathcal N(\mu, \sigma^2), \mu \in {\mathop{\mathbb R}}, \sigma \gt 0$
Mean $\mu$
Variance $\sigma^2$
PDF $f_X (x) = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2 \pi \sigma^2}} e^{-\frac{(x-\mu)^2}{2 \sigma^2}}$, $x \in {\mathop{\mathbb R}}$
