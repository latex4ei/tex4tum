## Scenarios
* choose $k$ elements from a total of $n$ elements
* distribute $k$ elements on $n$ fields


|	          |  Ordered      | Unordered               |
|-------------|:-------------:|:------------------------:|
| repeat      | $\displaystyle n^k$      |  $\displaystyle \binom{n+k-1}{k}$    |
| distinct    | $\displaystyle \frac{n!}{(n-k)!}$ | $\displaystyle  \binom{n}{k}$ |
