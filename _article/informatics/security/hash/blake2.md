BLAKE2 is a cryptographic hash function based on Dan Bernstein's ChaCha stream cipher.


| Variant  | Block  | State  | Rounds |   Output |
|----------|-------:|-------:|-------:|---------:|
| BLAKE2b  |  512 b | 1024 b |   12   | variable |
| BLAKE2s  |  256 b |  512 b |   10   | variable |
