SPI is serial bus.


## Specification

| Signals | |
|------|---|
| SCLK | Serial Clock |
| MOSI | Master Out, Slave In |
| MISO | Master In, Slave Out |
| <font style="text-decoration: overline;">SS</font> | ¬Slave Select |
