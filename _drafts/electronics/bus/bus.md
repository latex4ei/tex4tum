A Bus is a communication system that transfers data between devices. Data is sent over a medium (e.g. wires) and structured according to a certain protocol.


## Serial Buses
* [SPI](spi.html)
* [I2C](i2c.html)
* [USART](usart.html)
* [USB](usb.html)
